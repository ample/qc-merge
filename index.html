What is RefreshCincy?

RefreshCincy is a group of like-minded designers, developers, copywriters and other technologists interested in getting together, talking about technology and possibly drinking a few beers.

We meet once a month and discuss a trending topic or two of the day while engaging in some healthy debates about where the web is headed. So come on down, grab a seat and geek out with us.
